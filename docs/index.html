Cloud Developer Platform

Tailor your customers’ cloud experience around
the features that set you apart

Traditional enterprise solutions are a thing of the past

Cloud workloads currently represent a strong, and increasing, majority of all data center workloads worldwide.

VMware’s network of cloud providers collectively represent the 4th largest hyperscaler in terms of deployed workloads. And VMware Cloud Director is the preferred UI for delivering the capabilities of the cloud to service providers, and to their tenants. If you want your software in the hands of more paying customers, one of the best available routes to market today is to make that software a cloud service offering.

Set yourself apart

A successful cloud offering is more than just an individual product or suite of products

A successful cloud provider must have the ability to set themselves apart from other providers by tailoring their customers’ cloud experience around the features that set the provider apart. And the platform must allow the provider to easily entitle and monetize those differentiated experiences. Several basic tenets have driven VMware Cloud Director’s architecture and its popularity and growth over the past decade plus. One of these is that a successful cloud offering is more than just an individual product or suite of products. It is an entire and diverse ecosystem of features and functionality that must all work seamlessly together.
