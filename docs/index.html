Traditional enterprise solutions are a thing of the past

Cloud workloads currently represent a strong, and increasing, majority of all data center workloads worldwide.

VMware’s network of cloud providers collectively represent the 4th largest hyperscaler in terms of deployed workloads. And VMware Cloud Director is the preferred UI for delivering the capabilities of the cloud to service providers, and to their tenants. If you want your software in the hands of more paying customers, one of the best available routes to market today is to make that software a cloud service offering.

Set yourself apart

A successful cloud offering is more than just an individual product or suite of products

A successful cloud provider must have the ability to set themselves apart from other providers by tailoring their customers’ cloud experience around differentiated features and services. And the platform must allow the provider to easily entitle and monetize those differentiated experiences. Several basic principles have driven VMware Cloud Director’s architecture, its popularity and growth over the past decade. The main principle is that a successful cloud offering is more than just an individual product or suite of products. It is an entire diverse ecosystem of features and functionality that must all work together seamlessly.
